About Genie New World

Discover our mission, vision, and the values that drive us to empower young innovators

Our Story

Genie New World was founded in 2020 with a vision to bridge the gap between traditional education and the rapidly evolving technological landscape. We recognized that young students needed more than just textbook knowledge - they needed critical thinking skills, creativity, and innovation mindset to thrive in the new world.

Today, we're a global community of 50,000+ young innovators across 120 countries, all working together to shape the future through technology and creative thinking.

Our Vision

To create a world where every young person is empowered to think critically, create fearlessly, and innovate solutions for global challenges.

Our Mission

To provide youth with the mindset, skills, community, and resources needed to translate imagination into tangible projects that solve real-world problems.

Our Target

We work with students aged 15-25 who show passion for technology, creativity, and making a positive impact in their communities.

Core Values

  • Critical Thinking - We question assumptions and analyze information deeply
  • Creativity - We encourage unconventional thinking and new ideas
  • Innovation - We transform ideas into tangible solutions
  • Collaboration - We believe great things happen when we work together
  • Empowerment - We equip youth with tools to create change
