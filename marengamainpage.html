About MIC

Marenga Investment Company Limited (MIC Ltd) is a private company limited by shares, incorporated in the United Republic of Tanzania.

Established in 2007, MIC has grown from a small retail business to a diversified company with operations in production, transportation, distribution, and retail. With over 21 years of experience, we are committed to providing quality services and products to our customers.

Our Services

MIC provides a wide range of services with commitment, integrity, and creativity

Transport & Distribution

We provide reliable transport services for goods across Tanzania and neighboring countries with our fleet of quality trucks.

Production

Our milling and bakery operations produce high-quality products including "Sembe Jogoo la Afrika" maize flour, bread, and cakes.

Wholesale & Retail

We offer a wide range of quality products including sugar, cooking oil, soap, detergents, and more through our wholesale and retail channels.

Packing Services

We pack various products including rice, sugar, groundnuts, and flours in different sizes from 1kg to 25kg packages.

Agri-Business

We purchase agricultural products directly from farmers and engage in farming of maize, beans, and vegetables.

Import & Export

We engage in import and export business, trading products between Tanzania and neighboring countries.
